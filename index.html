Experience

Junior After Sales Engineer

Magnus Marine

The company is specialized in design and manufacturing shore to ship power converters in the superyacht industry. Our systems use rectifiers and inverters to convert the dock’s grid supply and make sure there is constant and undisturbed power feeding the ship’s sensitive electronics equipment as well as all the other important systems which are needed when in dock. The converters ensure protection to the ship’s electronics from power surges or unstable grid power and can use any supply from around the world without worrying about the voltage or frequency difference.
My role as an After Sales Engineer is to visit different sites and commission installations of our shore power converters as well as servicing and attending breakdowns and fault-find/repair existing systems on ships. When back at our base in England I take part in pioneering future product improvements and the testing process of the converters before they are shipped to our customers across the world. Some of my day to day duties are:

  • Testing and comissioning shore power converters
  • Servicing, warranty work and fault-finding breakdowns on existing systems
  • Writing documentation and test reports after comissioning
  • Reading and interpreting electrical schematics
